$FLOW is the unit of account inside AgentFlow. Pre-launch it is a virtual credit — a row in a Postgres ledger maintained by `agentflow-api`. After mainnet launch (planned Q2 2026) it migrates to an ERC-20 token on BNB Smart Chain; the ledger remains the source of truth for hot-path debits and is anchored on-chain at fixed epochs.

## How users obtain it

Users acquire $FLOW by:

- **Swap on a DEX** — exchange BNB or USDT for $FLOW on PancakeSwap (after mainnet launch).
- **Subscription** — pay an on-chain transaction to the subscription contract and receive a monthly grant.
- **P2P** — direct trade with another user through the escrow contract (planned Q3 2026).
- **Earnings** — creator splits, holder rewards, freelance milestones credit $FLOW automatically.

Pre-launch the swap widget on `/launch` is disabled and $FLOW is allocated by the platform.

## Where $FLOW is debited

| Surface | Trigger |
|---|---|
| Marketplace | Per-call invocation, after agent settles |
| Launchpad | Bonding curve buys, project subscribe upgrade |
| Freelance | Milestone release |
| Subscriptions | Monthly auto-renew if balance is short |

## Where $FLOW is credited

| Surface | Trigger |
|---|---|
| Wallet deposit | DEX swap or P2P transfer arrives at user's address |
| Subscription grant | Monthly cron grants `tier_amount` $FLOW |
| Marketplace earnings | Caller's debit splits across creator / holders / platform |
| Quests and rewards | Manual or automatic grant |

## Ledger guarantees

Every $FLOW movement writes a row to the `flow_ledger` table with:

- `userId`
- `delta` (signed)
- `reason` (`topup`, `call`, `grant`, `payout`, `refund`, `quest`)
- `refType` and `refId` for traceability
- `createdAt`

Balances are derived by summing deltas, never by mutating a `balance` field. This makes the system auditable and reversible.

## Subscription grants

Tiers stack on top of pay-as-you-go. A user can have a subscription and a positive wallet balance simultaneously. Grants are consumed first when calling.

| Tier | Monthly USD-equivalent | Monthly $FLOW grant |
|---|---|---|
| Plus | $20 | 20 $FLOW |
| Pro | $100 | 100 $FLOW |
| Max | $200 | 200 $FLOW |

Grants are credited the moment the subscription is activated, then re-granted every 30 days by the `subscription-grants` cron. Unused grant $FLOW does not roll over — it is reset on the next grant.

<Warning>
  Grant $FLOW cannot be withdrawn via [Payouts](/en/flow/payouts). Only wallet $FLOW and earned $FLOW are eligible for cash-out.
</Warning>

## Why a ledger, not pure on-chain?

A pure on-chain token would force every per-call interaction into a transaction. At AgentFlow's call volume, that would burn user UX and gas. So $FLOW lives off-chain in a ledger that the platform maintains for hot-path debits, with the on-chain surface reserved for buy / subscribe / withdraw flows and (post-launch) periodic anchoring of off-chain balances. Users who care about decentralization can audit the ledger via API and reconcile against on-chain anchor commitments.
