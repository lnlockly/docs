description: "Connect a wallet, pick a template, deploy your first token through the LaunchpadFactory in under five minutes."
---

This guide walks you from a blank wallet to a token deployed on BSC testnet. You will need an EVM wallet (MetaMask, Rabby, RainbowKit-supported) with the BSC testnet network added.

<Steps titleSize="h3">
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Open [agentflow.website](https://agentflow.website) and click **Connect Wallet**. AgentFlow uses Sign-In With Ethereum (SIWE): the server returns a nonce, you sign it, the server issues a JWT cookie. No on-chain transaction is required to sign in.

    Alternatively, sign in with Telegram via [@AIAgentFlow_bot](https://t.me/AIAgentFlow_bot). The bot issues the same JWT cookie.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get testnet USDT">
    Every token launched through the factory is paired with `paymentToken`. On BSC testnet that is `MockUSDT` at [`0xD8bD...F04B`](https://testnet.bscscan.com/address/0xD8bDfAAA00496EdE053210f2bcB0702C312FF04B). The Cabinet has a faucet button that mints you 1000 MockUSDT for testing. You will also need a small amount of testnet BNB for gas — grab it from any public BSC testnet faucet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a template">
    Open the [Launchpad](https://agentflow.website/launchpad/new). Two templates are registered:

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="virtuals" icon="chart-line">
        Bonding curve `x·y=k`. Trades pre-graduation on the built-in curve, then liquidity migrates to PancakeSwap V2 (BSC) at threshold. LP burned to `0xdead`. Best for memecoin-style or agent-of-marketplace tokens.
      </Card>
      <Card title="dpnm" icon="lock">
        Closed system. Algorithmic price `pool / supply`. Daily buy limit, income limit `1:2`, 3-branch × 10-level phenomenal tree with spillover, GWT fee compensation. **Never graduates to a DEX** — token lives inside the protocol contract forever, backed 100% by USDT.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Fill name, ticker, and template-specific parameters. The Cabinet calls `factory.launch(template_id, params)` from your wallet. EIP-1167 minimal proxy clones cost under `$0.50` total on BSC. Within one block your token is live.

    See [Create a Token](/en/launchpad/create-token) for the full parameter schema per template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="(Optional) Buy $FLOW">
    The platform's own token, `$FLOW`, is a `dpnm` instance. Buy a position to test the buy/sell flow with daily limit + income limit:

    1. Approve `FlowProtocol` to spend USDT on your behalf.
    2. Call `FlowProtocol.activate(referrer)` with `10 USDT` to register on the phenomenal tree.
    3. Call `FlowProtocol.buy(amount)` to acquire `$FLOW` tokens.

    `$FLOW` and `FlowProtocol` addresses live on the [Deployments](/en/onchain/deployments) page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
