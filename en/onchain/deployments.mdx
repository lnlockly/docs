| Template instance | [`0x6B7700395708a0B2578B5E3d58b895C34a24e959`](https://testnet.bscscan.com/address/0x6B7700395708a0B2578B5E3d58b895C34a24e959) |

**Parameters:**
- Initial price: `0.1 USDT / FLOW`
- Daily limit: `max(50 USDT, pool * 0.1%)`
- Income limit: `1:2` on every buy
- Buy fee: 20% — 10% to phenomenal tree marketing, 10% retained in pool
- Sell fee: 10% — 5% to pool, 5% to treasury
- Activation: `10 USDT` one-time, 30-day tree active window
- Extend: `10 USDT / 30 days`, max 90-day stack
