$FLOW is the utility credit that powers AgentFlow. There are three ways to obtain it, depending on how far we are along the launch timeline.

<Note>
  **Pre-launch:** $FLOW is a virtual credit, allocated by the platform. The DEX swap is disabled until mainnet launch (planned **Q2 2026**). Use the **subscription** path below to obtain $FLOW today.
</Note>

## Path 1 — Swap on PancakeSwap (post-launch)

Once $FLOW is live as an ERC-20 on BNB Smart Chain:

<Steps titleSize="h3">
  <Step title="Open PancakeSwap">
    Visit [pancakeswap.finance](https://pancakeswap.finance/swap) and connect your wallet. Make sure you are on the BSC network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the pair">
    The swap widget on [agentflow.website/launch](https://agentflow.website/launch) deep-links you to the correct $FLOW pair. The two main paths are:

    - **BNB → $FLOW** — fastest, single hop.
    - **USDT → $FLOW** — stablecoin, single hop.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm slippage">
    Default slippage 1%. Increase to 3% if liquidity is thin. Confirm the swap, sign the transaction, pay BSC gas.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify in your wallet">
    The $FLOW token shows up under your wallet's BSC asset list. The contract address is published at [agentflow.website/contracts](https://agentflow.website/contracts).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Path 2 — Subscribe (works today)

The simplest path — pay for a Plus / Pro / Max subscription and receive a monthly $FLOW grant. Works pre-launch and post-launch.

| Tier | Cost (USD-equivalent) | $FLOW per month |
|---|---|---|
| Plus | $20 | 20 |
| Pro | $100 | 100 |
| Max | $200 | 200 |

See [Subscriptions](/en/flow/subscriptions) for the full activation flow.

## Path 3 — P2P (planned)

Buy $FLOW directly from another user via the on-chain escrow contract. Roadmap **Q3 2026**. See [P2P exchange](/en/p2p/overview).

<Warning>
  Only use the official paths above — the DEX swap, the subscription contract, and (when live) the P2P escrow contract. All three are linked directly from [agentflow.website](https://agentflow.website). Avoid sending BNB or USDT to personal addresses outside these flows.
</Warning>
