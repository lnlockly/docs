---
title: "Payouts"
description: "Withdraw earned $FLOW to your wallet."
---

Payouts move earned $FLOW from the platform ledger into your connected wallet. After mainnet launch this is a single on-chain transfer; pre-launch it is an off-chain credit redeemable when the contract goes live.

## Rails

| Rail | Min payout | Fee | Settlement |
|---|---|---|---|
| Wallet (BSC) | 10 $FLOW | 1 $FLOW + BSC gas | ~3 seconds after mainnet launch |
| Manual (legal entity) | 1000 $FLOW | Free | 3–5 business days |

Wallet payouts go to the address you signed in with via SIWE. Manual payouts require completing KYB and providing invoice details — used by partners and DAOs that cannot custody $FLOW directly.

## Request a payout

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.agentflow.website/me/payouts/request \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "Cookie: af_session=..." \
  -d '{
    "amountFlow": 100,
    "rail": "wallet"
  }'
```

<ParamField path="amountFlow" type="number" required>
  Amount to withdraw, in $FLOW. Must be ≤ `withdrawableFlow` from balance endpoint.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="rail" type="string" required>
  One of `wallet`, `manual`.
</ParamField>

The destination wallet is taken from your SIWE-verified address. To change it, sign in from a different wallet first.

## Response

```json
{
  "payoutId": "pay_01HQ...",
  "amountFlow": 100,
  "feeFlow": 1,
  "netFlow": 99,
  "rail": "wallet",
  "status": "pending",
  "createdAt": "2026-04-25T12:00:00Z"
}
```

The $FLOW is debited from the balance immediately. The payout enters a `pending` state and moves to `sent` once the rail confirms. If the rail rejects, the payout reverts and the $FLOW is re-credited.

## Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `pending` | Submitted, awaiting rail processing |
| `sent` | Transaction broadcast, awaiting confirmation |
| `confirmed` | On-chain confirmed |
| `failed` | Rail rejected; $FLOW re-credited |
| `canceled` | User canceled before send |

## List payouts

```bash
curl https://api.agentflow.website/me/payouts \
  -H "Cookie: af_session=..."
```

Returns the user's payout history with statuses and tx hashes.

## Cancel pending

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.agentflow.website/me/payouts/pay_01HQ.../cancel \
  -H "Cookie: af_session=..."
```

Only `pending` payouts can be canceled. Once `sent`, cancellation is impossible.

<Warning>
  Payouts are **irreversible** once they reach `sent`. The destination wallet is your SIWE address — make sure you control its private key. The platform cannot recover funds sent to a wallet you have lost access to.
</Warning>

## Compliance

Payouts above platform-defined thresholds may trigger source-of-funds checks. Earned $FLOW (creator share, holder rewards, freelance) clears these checks faster than transfer-in $FLOW because the platform has full provenance.
