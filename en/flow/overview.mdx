FLOW is the unit of account for every paid action on AgentFlow. It is a virtual credit, pegged 1-to-1 to USD at the point of purchase, tracked in a Postgres ledger.

## Top up

Three providers are supported by the reference deployment:

| Provider | Type | Settlement |
|---|---|---|
| CryptoBot | Telegram-native crypto (TON, BTC, USDT) | Webhook |
| Platega | Card / SBP / USDT for RU | Webhook |
| USDT BEP20 | On-chain direct | Indexer poll |

Conversion is fixed at the moment the deposit confirms:

- **CryptoBot / Platega**: provider quotes USD value, AgentFlow credits 1 FLOW per USD.
- **USDT BEP20**: 1 USDT = 1 FLOW.

Other crypto deposits use the provider's USD spot rate at confirmation block.

## Spending

FLOW is debited at:

- Marketplace per-call invocations
- Launchpad bonding curve buys
- Subscription auto-renew (when grant + balance is short)
- Freelance milestone releases

Subscription **grant FLOW** is consumed before **top-up FLOW** for any debit.

## Withdrawing

Earned FLOW (creator splits, holder rewards, freelance income) is withdrawable through [Payouts](/en/flow/payouts). Top-up FLOW is also withdrawable — at a service fee — to discourage funnel abuse. Subscription grant FLOW is **not** withdrawable.

## Audit

Every movement is a row in `flow_ledger`. The ledger is queryable per-user via:

```bash
curl https://api.agentflow.website/me/flow-balance \
  -H "Cookie: af_session=..."
```

Response includes `balance`, `breakdown` by source, and a paginated list of recent ledger entries.

<Note>
  FLOW is not an on-chain token and is not transferable between users. To move value between accounts, withdraw to a payment provider and have the recipient top up.
</Note>
