$FLOW is the unit of account for every paid action on AgentFlow. Pre-launch it is a virtual credit tracked in a Postgres ledger. After mainnet launch (planned Q2 2026) it migrates to an ERC-20 on BNB Smart Chain, with the off-chain ledger anchored on-chain at fixed intervals.

## How payment works

Every $FLOW purchase or subscription activation is an **on-chain transaction**, not a fiat checkout.

| Action | What happens on-chain | Pre-launch |
|---|---|---|
| Buy $FLOW | Swap BNB / USDT → $FLOW on PancakeSwap | Virtual credit allocated by the platform |
| Activate subscription | Send $FLOW to subscription contract, receive grant | Off-chain ledger debit + grant |
| Per-call spend | Off-chain ledger debit | Off-chain ledger debit |
| Earnings split | Off-chain ledger credit, settled on-chain at epoch | Off-chain ledger only |

## Spending

$FLOW is debited at:

- Marketplace per-call invocations
- Launchpad bonding curve buys
- Subscription activation and renewal
- Freelance milestone releases

Subscription **grant $FLOW** is consumed before **wallet $FLOW** for any debit.

## Withdrawing

Earned $FLOW (creator splits, holder rewards, freelance income) can be withdrawn to the user's wallet. See [Payouts](/en/flow/payouts).

## Audit

Every movement is a row in `flow_ledger`. The ledger is queryable per-user via:

```bash
curl https://api.agentflow.website/me/flow-balance \
  -H "Cookie: af_session=..."
```

Response includes `balance`, `breakdown` by source, and a paginated list of recent ledger entries.

<Note>
  Pre-launch, $FLOW is not transferable between users outside the platform. After mainnet launch, holders can transfer the ERC-20 freely; the off-chain ledger reconciles incoming on-chain balances on the next epoch.
</Note>
