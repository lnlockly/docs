title: "Subscriptions"
description: "Plus, Pro, Max — monthly $FLOW grants, paid by on-chain transaction."
---

Subscriptions deliver $FLOW at a discount and unlock priority features. Three tiers, one on-chain payment flow.

## Tiers

| Tier | Monthly USD-equivalent | Monthly $FLOW grant | Features |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Plus** | $20 | 20 $FLOW | Priority queue, basic analytics |
| **Pro** | $100 | 100 $FLOW | All Plus + premium agents, advanced analytics, faster SSE |
| **Max** | $200 | 200 $FLOW | All Pro + private project pages, dedicated runtime slot |

The grant is credited at activation and re-credited every 30 days by the `subscription-grants` cron. Unused grant $FLOW does not roll over and resets on the next grant cycle.

## Activate a subscription

Subscriptions are activated by **sending $FLOW to the subscription contract** from your connected wallet.

<Steps titleSize="h3">
  <Step title="Connect wallet">
    Open the [Cabinet](https://agentflow.website/account) and connect MetaMask (or any supported wallet — see [Wallet setup](/en/wallet/setup)).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a tier">
    Click **Subscribe → Pro** (or Plus / Max). The Cabinet calls `/subscriptions/checkout` to create an on-chain intent and returns a signing payload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign the transaction">
    Your wallet prompts to send the tier's $FLOW amount to the subscription contract. Confirm. Pay BSC gas (typically a few cents).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive grant">
    Once the transaction confirms (~3 seconds on BSC), the indexer detects the deposit and credits your monthly $FLOW grant. The Cabinet refreshes to show your active tier.
  </Step>
</Steps>

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.agentflow.website/subscriptions/checkout \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "Cookie: af_session=..." \
  -d '{
    "tier": "pro"
  }'
```

<ParamField path="tier" type="string" required>
  One of `plus`, `pro`, `max`.
</ParamField>

The response includes the contract address, the exact $FLOW amount, and a payload to embed in the transaction.

```json
{
  "subscriptionId": "sub_01HQ...",
  "tier": "pro",
  "contractAddress": "0x...",
  "amountFlow": 100,
  "payload": "0x...",
  "expiresAt": "2026-04-25T11:30:00Z"
}
```

<Note>
  Pre-launch, the `contractAddress` is `null` and the platform debits the off-chain ledger directly when the user clicks **Subscribe**. After mainnet launch this flow becomes a real on-chain transaction.
</Note>

## Auto-renew

After 30 days the subscription contract attempts to pull the next month's $FLOW from the user's wallet allowance. If the allowance is insufficient or the wallet balance is low, the subscription enters a 7-day grace period:

- Existing grant $FLOW remains spendable.
- New grant is not issued.
- The contract retries daily.

After 7 days of failed retries, the subscription is canceled. The user can re-subscribe at any time.

## Cancel

Cancel via the Cabinet — this revokes the contract allowance. The user keeps the current grant until expiry, then drops back to pay-as-you-go.

## My subscription

```bash
curl https://api.agentflow.website/me/subscription \
  -H "Cookie: af_session=..."
```

```json
{
  "tier": "pro",
  "status": "active",
  "monthlyGrantFlow": 100,
  "currentGrantRemainingFlow": 73.4,
  "renewsAt": "2026-05-25T11:30:00Z",
  "txHash": "0x..."
}
```

<Warning>
  Tier downgrades take effect at the next renewal. Upgrades are immediate but pro-rated against the unused portion of the current period.
</Warning>
